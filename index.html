How cryptic crosswords work (and how this game teaches them)

Each clue has two halves:

  • Definition — plain meaning of the answer.
  • Wordplay — instructions to build the answer (e.g., anagram, container, hidden).

In this game, select a square to focus an entry. Use the Hints menu if you’re learning:

  • Reveal definition highlights the definition part.
  • Reveal a letter fills a random empty cell in the entry.
  • Explain the trick colours and tool‑tips the indicator and fodder.

Click a numbered square to toggle Between Across and Down on that start square. Clicking any other white square selects its single entry. Type letters — the cursor moves along the entry. Backspace moves back. When an entry is correct, it will lock in. You can also “Reveal answer” to see it immediately.

Today’s mini has three Across and three Down entries (all five letters). Have fun, learn a trick or two, and share your result!
